Why Blood Matters

Blood Facts & Figures

Blood is the river of life. These are the numbers behind every donation that can change the world.

🩸4.7–5.5 L
Blood in the Human Body

An average adult carries 4.7–5.5 litres of blood, accounting for roughly 7–8% of total body weight.

❤️3
Lives Saved Per Donation

A single whole blood donation is separated into red cells, plasma, and platelets — each saving a different life.

🔬25 Tr
Red Blood Cells in the Body

25 trillion red blood cells circulate at any moment, each surviving about 120 days before being replaced.

⏱️10 min
Duration of Donation

The actual blood draw takes just 8–10 minutes. The entire process including registration takes about 45 minutes.

🔄56 days
Time to Donate Again

You can donate whole blood every 56 days (8 weeks). Your body replenishes plasma within 24 hours.

🏥1.2 Cr
Annual Donations Needed (India)

India needs 1.2 crore units of blood per year but collects only about 90 lakh — a critical gap.
