Critical Windows

Organ Viability Timeline

Time from procurement to transplant. Every minute matters.

❤️
Heart
4 – 6 Hours
Most time-critical organ. Requires dedicated air transport and pre-matched recipient on standby.
🫁
Lungs
4 – 6 Hours
Transported in specially cooled containers. Size matching with the recipient's chest cavity is essential.
🔴
Pancreas
12 – 24 Hours
Usually transplanted together with kidney. Blood type and size matching required for success.
🟤
Liver
12 – 24 Hours
The liver's ability to regenerate makes it uniquely versatile. Can be split for two recipients in some cases.
🫘
Kidneys
Up to 72 Hours
The longer window allows for cross-region matching. Machine perfusion can extend viability further.
👁️
Corneas
Up to 14 Days
Stored in eye banks at controlled temperatures. The generous window allows wide geographic distribution.
